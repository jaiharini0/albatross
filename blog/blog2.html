Business Loan Eligibility Guide

Business loans help entrepreneurs expand operations, manage cash flow, and invest in growth. Understanding eligibility criteria helps you prepare better.

Key Eligibility Factors

  • Business vintage of at least 2–3 years
  • Stable annual turnover
  • Good credit score (CIBIL 700+)
  • Proper GST & IT filings

Documents Required

PAN, Aadhaar, GST returns, bank statements, financial statements, and business registration documents.

How to Improve Approval Chances

Maintain accurate books of accounts, reduce existing liabilities, and consult a financial advisor before applying.
