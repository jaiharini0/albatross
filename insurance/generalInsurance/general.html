General Insurance Eligibility & Documentation

Simple eligibility criteria for asset and risk protection

  • Applicant Type: Individuals, vehicle owners, property owners, business owners, and travelers
  • Age Requirement: 18 years and above (age limits may apply for specific policies like motor or travel)
  • Insurable Interest: Applicant must have legal ownership or financial interest in the insured asset
  • Risk Profile: Based on asset type, usage, location, and risk exposure
  • Policy Type: Motor, Home, Travel, Commercial, or Personal Accident insurance
  • Sum Insured: Determined by asset value, coverage selected, and insurer guidelines
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport)
  • Proof of Address
  • Asset Documents (RC book, Property papers, Invoice, or Travel details)
  • Policy Proposal Form (filled and signed)
  • Previous Policy Copy (for renewals, if applicable)
  • Photographs of Asset / Vehicle (if required)

Our advisors assist with documentation, valuation, and insurer coordination for smooth policy issuance.
