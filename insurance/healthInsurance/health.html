Health Insurance Eligibility & Documentation

Simple eligibility for medical protection

  • Applicant Type: Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, business owners, and homemakers
  • Age Requirement: 18 – 65 years (varies by insurer and policy type)
  • Income Criteria: Regular income source preferred for higher coverage eligibility
  • Health Condition: Medical history and lifestyle habits assessed during underwriting
  • Policy Type: Eligibility may vary for Term, Endowment, ULIP, and Whole Life plans
  • Coverage Amount: Based on age, income, health profile, and financial responsibilities
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport)
  • Proof of Address
  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate / Passport / PAN)
  • Income Proof (Salary slips / ITR / Bank Statements – if required)
  • Medical Reports (if requested by the insurer)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph
